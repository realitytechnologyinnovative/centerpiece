About CentrePiece

Driving Strategic Health Transformation

CentrePiece is a specialist consultancy and execution arm of the Fatah Zango Group, founded 2 and half decade ago, the arm focused on designing, managing, and scaling transformative healthcare projects across Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa. We work with hospitals, governments, multilateral institutions, and private developers to provide project strategy, implementation support, and performance monitoring in healthcare delivery. Our experience spans primary care expansion, infrastructure strategy, pharmaceutical systems, and health innovation ecosystems.

Services:

  • Health Systems Strengthening Consulting
  • Hospital & Facility Master Planning
  • Health Policy Advisory & Framework Design
  • Program Management for Large-Scale Health Projects
  • Monitoring & Evaluation for Health Outcomes
  • PPP (Public-Private Partnership) Structuring in Health

About the CEO

Dr. Mohammed Musa Zango

Dr. Mohammed Musa Zango is a visionary leader in healthcare administration with over 34 years of service at NNPC Medical Services Limited. Renowned for driving innovation, integrity, and operational excellence, he has led transformative projects—including the establishment of multispecialist hospitals, pharmaceutical facilities, and diagnostic centres across Nigeria.


As the former Managing Director of NNPC Medical Services and Group Executive Director, Ventures, his strategic initiatives have reshaped healthcare delivery and infrastructure. A PharmD holder with additional degrees in health planning and management, Dr. Zango is a multiple-award-winning professional and a Fellow of several esteemed institutions.


His passion for quality care, innovation, and public health continues to shape Nigeria’s medical and pharmaceutical landscape.
